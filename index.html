The Connection Between Tech And Motherhood

Technology has a significant impact on motherhood(or even parenthood as a whole), offering both opportunities and challenges. It can provide access to information, connection, support networks, empowering mothers with resources for education, healthcare, and work-life balance, ultimately enhancing their well-being and their children's development; however also contributes to feelings of comparison,anxiety, and potential distractions from family time. I, however, believe that when utilised properly technology can have a greater postive impact rather than a negative one. According to ScienceDirect.com, recent research suggests that the use of smartphone apps may increase a mother's capacity for 'mind-mindedness,' which may enable a secure infant-caregiver attachment (Larkin et al., 2019) and indicate that the use of digital media may have positive consequences for the mother, child, and their developing relationship.

      • Apps and wearable devices can help mothers track their health, monitor their children's well-being, and make informed decisions about their care.
